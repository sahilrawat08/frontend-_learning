Basic Video with Multiple Sources

Auto-playing Muted Video

Attributes Used:
  • autoplay - Starts playing automatically
  • muted - Required for autoplay in most browsers
  • loop - Repeats the video
  • controls - Shows play/pause buttons
  • poster - Thumbnail before playing

Audio with Custom Styling

Audio Attributes:
  • controls - Shows audio controls
  • autoplay - Auto-plays (avoid for UX)
  • loop - Repeats audio
  • muted - Starts muted
  • preload - none, metadata, auto
